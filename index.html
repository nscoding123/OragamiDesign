Cool Facts about Camels!

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.

Cool Facts about Chameleons!

  • Chameleons Have Almost 360 Degree Vision
  • The longest is Malagasy giant or Oustalet’s chameleon up to 60 centimeters (23 inches).
  • The shortest is Brookesia micra, which reaches 1.5 centimeters (0.6 inches).

Cool Facts about Pigeons!

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons can travel 400-600 miles away from its home and can amazingly return within the day.
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals often seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.

Cool Facts about Teddy Bears!

  • Studies show an estimated 60-70 percent of children in the U.S. and U.K. show attachment to a teddy bear or blanket.
  • The first teddy bear went on sale in February of 1903.
  • The teddy bear is named after Theodore Roosevelt, the 26th president of the United States.

Cool Facts about Pandas!

  • They have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).

Cool Facts about Cicadas!

  • Cicadas can’t eat or bite because they lack jaws.
  • Cicadas are harmless and can’t bite or sting.
  • It takes 17 years for cicadas to come out!
